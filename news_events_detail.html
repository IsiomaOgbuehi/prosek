October 27, 2020 | In Articles | By Moebetter

Inspiration Park and Farm aims to be a destination that combines farming with visual arts and farmland innovation, over its 20 hectares, to create a unique destination in Lagos for tourists and locals off the Lekki-Epe expressway.

Inspiration Park and Farm will be the first of its kind, in Nigeria a sustainable farm and artistic community living side by side. The project is focused on taking advantage of under-utilized arable land in the pre zoned farm communities to grow food. To create a sustainable multi functional and multidisciplinary environment that merges the current two fastest growing industry drivers for the Nigerian national economy, outside oil.

Conceived by Yinka Shonibare CBE, RA in 2018: the project is unlike anything realized in Nigeria to date. It will include an event space, cafe, amphitheatre, sculpture garden, visual artist studios and accommodation, an artist-in-residence program, with farming integrated throughout each arm of the project.

Farming methods will include a mixture of the traditional soil-based farming and more innovative farming techniques that incorporate aero-ponics and hydroponics.
